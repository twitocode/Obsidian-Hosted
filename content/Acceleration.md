# Acceleration
How quickly an object's [[velocity]] changes over time. It is also the the **rate of change** of [[velocity]].
<!--ID: 1718370433205-->


$$\vec{a}_{av} = \frac{\vec{v}_{f} - \vec{v}_{i}}{\Delta t}$$
$$a(t) = v'(t) = s''(t)$$
It is measured in $m/s^2$
